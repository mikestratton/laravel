Technical

Our software architecture is based on the Model-View-Controller pattern. Our approach to each software/web project is to find the highest quality solution, in the most cost effective way. This is done by matching your specific software use case to the appropriate platform for application development. We support the development of web applications on the LAMP stack, with priority given to applications developed with Laravel. We also support content management systems such as Wordpress, Drupal and Joomla. Our front end development is driven by Bootstrap and jQuery.

If your business is successful, so are we. Increase your chances for succes by working with MVC Horse. Our development standards are driven by an education and experience in computer science and mathematics. We develop highly scalable, robust and efficient web applications.
